The Crisis

India is on fire.
And 92% of its people have nowhere to hide.

Extreme heat is no longer a seasonal inconvenience. Temperatures crossed 52°C in Delhi in 2024. Heatwaves now last weeks, not days. 37 Indian cities simultaneously recorded above 45°C in a single season — and the government in Akola imposed Section 144, banning public gatherings because stepping outside had become life-threatening.

While the wealthy retreat to air conditioning, over 300 million households — daily wage earners, factory workers, students, elderly parents — are trapped inside homes that become ovens. Their walls absorb heat all day and radiate it all night. Sleep becomes impossible. Work becomes dangerous. And the only "solution" sold to them costs more electricity than they can afford.

"I couldn't sleep.
I couldn't study.
My walls were the problem."

This is the personal testimony of Aditya Ratnaparkhi, FlamTabX founder, recalling his years in student dormitories without AC — where indoor heat regularly exceeded survivable comfort levels during Indian summers.

His insight was simple: instead of fighting heat with energy, make the building itself the solution. Transform the wall from a passive heat-absorber into an active thermal management system. No electricity needed. No monthly bill. No carbon emissions. Just better materials.

That insight became FlamTabX — a climate-adaptive, bio-based coating that works with nature, not against it, to reduce indoor heat, eliminate dampness, and prevent the mold that makes unhealthy homes even more dangerous.

The breakthrough

COOLING
WITHOUT
CURRENT

01

Phase Change Materials

Embedded heat-managing compounds absorb excess thermal energy during peak afternoon heat and release it slowly after sunset — dramatically reducing indoor temperature spikes without a single watt of electricity.

Thermal buffering
02

Reflective + UV Shield

Specially formulated reflective components bounce solar radiation before it can be absorbed by walls and roofs — the first line of defence against heat entry. UV-protective additives ensure long-term durability in harsh Indian summers.

Solar rejection
03

Breathable Bio-Based Matrix

A CNSL (Cashew Nut Shell Liquid) bio-resin base derived from agricultural waste creates a breathable coating that allows moisture to escape — eliminating the damp walls and mold growth that make monsoon-season homes dangerously unhealthy.

Moisture control
04

Antimicrobial Protection

Built-in antimicrobial properties reduce bacterial and fungal growth on wall surfaces — an invisible but critical health intervention for the millions of families whose homes develop mold during India's humid seasons.

Indoor air quality
Application

Applied like paint.
Works like science.

FlamTabX requires no construction, no special equipment, and no specialist training. Any painter, any contractor, any home improvement team can apply it — creating instant, permanent passive cooling with a single coat.

01

Surface preparation

Standard wall or roof surface prepared by any local contractor — same as conventional paint application.

02

Single coat application

FlamTabX applied by brush, roller, or spray — identical process to conventional exterior paint. No special tools.

03

Curing & activation

Bio-resin matrix bonds to surface within hours. Phase change materials begin absorbing and regulating heat from first exposure to sunlight.

04

Continuous passive cooling

From day one, walls and roofs begin managing heat, moisture, and microbial growth — with no ongoing energy input or maintenance required.
